# Milestone Check-in Templates

Meeting agendas and assessment templates for 30/60/90 day milestone check-ins with rating frameworks.

---

## 30-Day Milestone Check-in

**Duration**: 45 minutes
**Participants**: Manager + New Hire
**Format**: 1:1 conversation, collaborative

### Pre-Meeting Preparation (Manager)

```markdown
# 30-Day Review Preparation: [New Hire Name]

## Data to Review (Before Meeting)

**Technical Metrics**:
- Linear issues completed: [X] (Target: [Y])
- PRs merged: [count]
- Average feedback rounds: [X.X] (Target: < 2)
- Test coverage: [X%] (Target: 80%+)
- Production bugs: [count] (Target: 0)

**Collaboration Metrics**:
- Code reviews given: [count]
- Questions asked: [frequency]
- Meeting participation: [observations]

**Qualitative Data**:
- Buddy feedback: [summary from buddy check-in]
- Team feedback: [summary from teammates]
- Manager observations: [your notes from 1:1s]

## Assessment

**Technical Progress**: [Exceeding / Meeting / Below expectations]
- Evidence: [specific examples]

**Team Integration**: [Exceeding / Meeting / Below expectations]
- Evidence: [specific examples]

**Trajectory**: [Strong / On track / Concerns]
- Evidence: [trend analysis]
```

### Meeting Agenda Template

#### Part 1: Progress Review (20 minutes)

**Manager Opens**:
```
"Let's review your first 30 days. I want to hear your perspective first, then I'll share mine."
```

**Questions for New Hire**:

1. **Technical Progress**:
   - "What have you shipped that you're proud of?"
   - "What's been the quality like? (How's code review going?)"
   - "Where are you still learning?"

2. **Team Integration**:
   - "How's the team integration going?"
   - "Are you comfortable asking questions?"
   - "Who have you connected with on the team?"

3. **Velocity**:
   - "Are you meeting your own expectations for productivity?"
   - "What's blocking you?"

#### Part 2: Feedback Exchange (15 minutes)

**Manager Asks**:
```
"Now I want to hear about your experience."
```

1. **What's going well?**
   - [Let them share wins and positive experiences]
   - [Celebrate specific accomplishments]

2. **What's challenging?**
   - [Let them share struggles without judgment]
   - [Problem-solve together]

3. **What support do you need?**
   - [Identify specific asks]
   - [Commit to providing resources]

**Manager Shares Feedback**:
```
"Here's my assessment of your first 30 days..."

**Strengths**:
- [Specific positive feedback with examples]
- [What they're doing well]

**Development Areas**:
- [Specific areas for growth with examples]
- [Coaching on how to improve]

**Overall**: [Exceeding / Meeting / Below expectations]
```

#### Part 3: Forward-Looking (10 minutes)

**Manager Sets Goals**:
```
"For the next 30 days, let's focus on..."

1. [Specific technical goal]
   - What: [description]
   - Why: [business/career reason]
   - Success metric: [how we'll measure]

2. [Specific collaboration goal]
   - What: [description]
   - Why: [team/culture reason]
   - Success metric: [how we'll measure]

3. [Specific stretch goal]
   - What: [description]
   - Why: [growth reason]
   - Success metric: [how we'll measure]
```

**Closing Questions**:
```
- "Do these goals feel achievable?"
- "Any questions about expectations?"
- "What would make the next 30 days successful for you?"
```

### 30-Day Rating Scale

**9-10/10 - Exceeding Expectations**:
- Velocity 20%+ above target
- Code quality exceptional (< 1.5 feedback rounds)
- Proactive and independent
- Example: "Alex shipped 8 features (target: 5), 100% test coverage, zero bugs"

**7-8/10 - Meeting Expectations**:
- Velocity at target ±10%
- Code quality good (< 2 feedback rounds)
- Collaborating well
- Example: "Jordan shipped 5 features (target: 5), good code quality, asking questions"

**5-6/10 - Below Expectations - Intervention Needed**:
- Velocity < 50% of target
- Code quality concerns (> 3 feedback rounds)
- Not asking questions (red flag)
- Example: "Sam shipped 3 features (target: 5), struggling with quality, isolated"
- **Action**: Create intervention plan, increase check-in frequency

**< 5/10 - Serious Concerns - Formal Action**:
- Velocity < 40% of target
- Multiple red flags
- No improvement trajectory
- **Action**: Structured improvement plan or PIP consideration

## 60-Day Milestone Check-in

**Duration**: 45 minutes
**Participants**: Manager + New Hire
**Format**: Performance discussion + Career planning

### Pre-Meeting Preparation (Manager)

```markdown
# 60-Day Review Preparation: [New Hire Name]

## Progress Analysis (30-day vs 60-day)

**Velocity Trend**:
- 30-day: [X%] of team average
- 60-day: [Y%] of team average
- Trend: [Improving / Flat / Declining]

**Scope Trend**:
- 30-day: [Simple tasks]
- 60-day: [Medium/Complex tasks?]
- Trend: [Growing / Flat]

**Quality Trend**:
- 30-day: [X.X] avg feedback rounds
- 60-day: [Y.Y] avg feedback rounds
- Trend: [Improving / Flat / Declining]

## Assessment

**Technical Depth**: [Strong / Growing / Concerns]
**Leadership** (if applicable): [Emerging / Not yet / N/A]
**Career Trajectory**: [Fast-track / On track / Concerns]
```

### Meeting Agenda Template

#### Part 1: Growth Assessment (20 minutes)

**Manager Opens**:
```
"You've been here 2 months. Let's talk about your growth trajectory."
```

**Questions for New Hire**:

1. **Technical Depth**:
   - "What complex problems have you solved recently?"
   - "Where do you feel like an expert now?"
   - "What's still challenging?"

2. **Leadership** (if applicable):
   - "Have you had opportunities to mentor or lead?"
   - "Are you contributing to design discussions?"
   - "What have you improved beyond your own tasks?"

3. **Self-Assessment**:
   - "How do you compare to your 30-day self?"
   - "What's improved most?"
   - "Where do you want to grow next?"

#### Part 2: Career Discussion (15 minutes)

**Manager Guides**:
```
"Let's talk about your career trajectory here."
```

1. **Career Aspirations**:
   - "What do you want to be known for?"
   - "What skills do you want to develop?"
   - "Where do you see yourself in 6-12 months?"

2. **Development Planning**:
   - "Here's how I see your strengths: [specific examples]"
   - "Here's where I see growth opportunities: [specific areas]"
   - "Here's how I can support you: [specific commitments]"

3. **Promotion Discussion** (if applicable):
   - "For [junior → mid / mid → senior], here's what you need to demonstrate..."
   - "Timeline: Realistically [X months] if trajectory continues"

#### Part 3: Performance Rating (10 minutes)

**Manager Provides Assessment**:
```
"Here's my assessment of your 60-day performance..."

**Technical**: [Rating 1-10]
- [Specific strengths with examples]
- [Specific areas for growth]

**Collaboration**: [Rating 1-10]
- [How they work with team]
- [Communication quality]

**Growth Trajectory**: [Rating 1-10]
- [30-day vs 60-day comparison]
- [Trend analysis]

**Overall Rating**: [X/10]
- [Exceeding / Meeting / Below expectations]
```

**Forward-Looking Goals**:
```
"For the next 30 days, focus on..."

1. [Technical stretch goal]
2. [Leadership opportunity]
3. [Career development goal]
```

### 60-Day Rating Scale

**9-10/10 - Exceptional - Fast-Track**:
- Velocity 100%+ of team average
- Leading medium/complex features
- Mentoring others or architectural contributions
- Example: "Jordan leading backend performance work, 85% latency reduction, mentoring 2 engineers"
- **Trajectory**: Promotion discussions, fast-track opportunities

**8-9/10 - Strong - Exceeding Expectations**:
- Velocity 90%+ of team average
- Owning medium features end-to-end
- Contributing to team beyond tasks
- Example: "Alex shipped user profile editor (complex), mentoring junior, speaking at tech talk"
- **Trajectory**: On track for promotion timeline

**7-8/10 - Solid - Meeting Expectations**:
- Velocity 70-90% of team average
- Growing technical depth
- Good team collaboration
- Example: "Casey shipped 6 features, code quality improving, team integration strong"
- **Trajectory**: Steady growth, meeting bar

**6-7/10 - Concerns - Intervention Needed**:
- Velocity flat or declining from 30-day
- No scope increase
- Behavioral concerns
- Example: "Sam's velocity hasn't improved (40% at 30-day, 45% at 60-day), isolated, defensive"
- **Action**: Structured 30-day improvement plan, daily check-ins, clear expectations

## 90-Day Formal Performance Review

**Duration**: 60 minutes
**Participants**: Manager + New Hire
**Format**: Formal performance review + Future planning

### Pre-Meeting Preparation (Manager)

```markdown
# 90-Day Performance Review: [New Hire Name]

## Complete Performance Analysis

**Technical Contributions** (30% weight):
- Features shipped: [list major accomplishments]
- Code quality: [assessment with metrics]
- Technical depth: [areas of expertise]
- Impact: [business/team impact]

**Collaboration** (30% weight):
- Code reviews: [quality and quantity]
- Mentoring: [who have you helped]
- Communication: [clarity and proactivity]
- Team culture: [positive contributions]

**Growth Trajectory** (40% weight):
- 30-day → 60-day → 90-day progression
- Velocity trend: [improving / flat / declining]
- Scope trend: [increasing / flat]
- Learning agility: [fast learner / steady / slow]

## Performance Rating Determination

**Final Rating**: [X/10]
- [Rationale with specific evidence]

**Salary/Promotion Decision**:
- [ ] Promote immediately
- [ ] On track for promotion at [X] months
- [ ] Meet expectations, standard trajectory
- [ ] Below expectations, improvement plan
- [ ] Not meeting bar, exit conversation
```

### Meeting Agenda Template

#### Part 1: Performance Assessment (30 minutes)

**Manager Opens**:
```
"This is your formal 90-day performance review. I've assessed your performance across technical contributions, collaboration, and growth trajectory."
```

**Technical Contributions**:
```
**Features Shipped**:
- [List major accomplishments with impact]
- Example: "User profile editor (2 weeks, complex), Query optimization (85% latency reduction)"

**Code Quality**:
- Average feedback rounds: [X.X]
- Test coverage: [X%]
- Production bugs: [count]
- Assessment: [Excellent / Good / Needs improvement]

**Technical Depth**:
- Expertise areas: [specific technologies]
- Complexity handling: [Simple / Medium / Complex]
- Assessment: [Strong / Growing / Developing]

**Impact**:
- [Business impact with metrics]
- [Team impact with examples]
```

**Collaboration**:
```
**Code Reviews**:
- Given: [count] reviews
- Quality: [Thorough / Helpful / Learning]

**Mentoring** (if applicable):
- Who: [names]
- Impact: [their growth]

**Communication**:
- Clarity: [Clear / Developing]
- Proactivity: [Proactive / Reactive]

**Team Culture**:
- [Positive contributions]
- [Culture fit assessment]
```

**Growth Trajectory**:
```
**Progression**:
- 30-day: [X%] velocity, [scope], [assessment]
- 60-day: [Y%] velocity, [scope], [assessment]
- 90-day: [Z%] velocity, [scope], [assessment]

**Trend**: [Strong upward / Steady / Flat / Declining]
**Learning Agility**: [Fast / Steady / Needs support]
```

#### Part 2: Performance Rating (10 minutes)

**Manager Delivers Rating**:
```
"Your overall performance rating is [X/10]."

**Rating Breakdown**:
- Technical: [X/10]
- Collaboration: [Y/10]
- Growth: [Z/10]
- **Overall**: [Final/10]

**What This Means**:
- [Exceeding / Meeting / Below expectations]
- [Specific implications]
```

### 90-Day Rating Scale & Outcomes

**9-10/10 - Exceptional - Promote or Fast-Track**:
```
Performance: Far exceeds expectations
Examples:
- Jordan: Tech Lead promotion, architectural impact, mentoring 2 engineers
- Velocity: 150% of team average + force multiplier

Outcome:
- Immediate promotion OR
- Fast-track timeline (6-12 months vs standard 18-24)
- Salary adjustment
- High-visibility projects
```

**8-9/10 - Strong - Exceeding Expectations**:
```
Performance: Exceeds expectations
Examples:
- Alex: 153% of target issues, accessibility leadership, mentoring
- Velocity: 100-120% of team average

Outcome:
- Standard promotion timeline (18-24 months)
- Positive performance rating
- Growing responsibilities
- Strong team member
```

**7-8/10 - Solid - Meeting Expectations**:
```
Performance: Meets expectations
Examples:
- Casey: 120% of target, good code quality, strong collaboration
- Velocity: 80-100% of team average

Outcome:
- Continue current trajectory
- Standard development plan
- No concerns
```

**6-7/10 - Concerns - Performance Improvement Plan**:
```
Performance: Below expectations
Examples:
- Sam: 60% of target, quality issues, isolation concerns
- Velocity: 50-70% of team average

Outcome:
- 30-day Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)
- Clear metrics and expectations
- Daily manager check-ins
- Decision point: 30 days (improve OR exit)
```

**< 6/10 - Not Meeting Bar - Exit Conversation**:
```
Performance: Significantly below expectations
Examples:
- Multiple red flags persist
- No improvement from interventions
- Not meeting minimum bar for role

Outcome:
- Transition out of company
- Severance package
- Support for job search
- Part ways professionally
```

#### Part 3: Future Planning (20 minutes)

**Career Trajectory**:
```
"Based on your performance, here's your career trajectory..."

**Promotion Timeline** (if applicable):
- [Junior → Mid]: [X] months
- [Mid → Senior]: [X] months
- Requirements: [specific criteria]

**Development Plan** (next 6 months):

1. **Technical Skills**:
   - Goal: [specific skill to develop]
   - Timeline: [X] months
   - Resources: [courses, projects, mentorship]

2. **Leadership Skills** (if applicable):
   - Goal: [specific leadership growth]
   - Timeline: [X] months
   - Opportunity: [specific projects]

3. **Career Growth**:
   - Goal: [broader career objective]
   - Timeline: [6-12 months]
   - Support: [manager commitments]
```

**Closing**:
```
"Questions about your rating or trajectory?"
"What would make the next 6 months successful for you?"
"I'm committed to supporting your growth. Let's check in monthly on progress."
```

### Post-Review Actions

**Documentation**:
- [ ] Performance review document saved to HR system
- [ ] Development plan documented in Linear
- [ ] Salary/promotion decisions processed

**Follow-up**:
- [ ] Monthly 1:1s continue
- [ ] Quarterly performance check-ins
- [ ] Annual performance review cycle
