## Chapter Checklist

### Story Progress
- [ ] Advances main plot
- [ ] Develops character arc
- [ ] Raises/answers questions
- [ ] Creates tension or stakes

### Craft Elements
- [ ] POV consistent throughout
- [ ] Sensory details grounded
- [ ] Dialogue distinct per character
- [ ] Balance of action, dialogue, interiority
- [ ] Show vs tell appropriate

### Pacing
- [ ] Opening hook within first paragraph
- [ ] Scenes have goal-conflict-outcome
- [ ] Closing hook to next chapter
- [ ] Varied sentence length for rhythm

### Revision Questions
- [ ] What is this chapter's purpose?
- [ ] Could it be cut? If not, why?
- [ ] What does reader learn/feel?
- [ ] Does character change, even slightly?
