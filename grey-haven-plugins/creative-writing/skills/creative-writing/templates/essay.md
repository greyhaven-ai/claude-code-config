## Revision Checklist

### Structure
- [ ] Opening hook within first 2-3 sentences
- [ ] Clear through-line (even if implicit)
- [ ] Each paragraph earns its place
- [ ] Builds to something (not just summarizes)
- [ ] Ending is earned, not tacked on

### Voice
- [ ] Sounds like a person, not a term paper
- [ ] "I" placement varied (not every paragraph opener)
- [ ] Sentence variety (length, structure)
- [ ] Specific > abstract

### Content
- [ ] Passes "so what?" test after each paragraph
- [ ] Balance of scene and reflection
- [ ] Concrete details anchor abstract ideas
- [ ] Reader learns something or feels something

### Polish
- [ ] Cut first paragraph? (often throat-clearing)
- [ ] Remove "very," "really," "just"
- [ ] Active voice where possible
- [ ] Read aloud for rhythm
