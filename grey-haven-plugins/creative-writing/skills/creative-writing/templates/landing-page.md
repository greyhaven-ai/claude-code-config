## Landing Page Checklist

### Clarity
- [ ] Can I understand the offer in 5 seconds?
- [ ] Is the CTA obvious and specific?
- [ ] One page, one goal?

### Persuasion
- [ ] Benefits over features?
- [ ] Social proof present?
- [ ] Objections addressed?
- [ ] Risk reversed?

### Conversion
- [ ] CTA above fold?
- [ ] CTA repeated after each section?
- [ ] Friction minimized (form fields, steps)?
- [ ] Mobile optimized?

### Copy
- [ ] Headline specific and benefit-focused?
- [ ] Short paragraphs and bullets?
- [ ] First person CTAs?
- [ ] No jargon or cleverness over clarity?
